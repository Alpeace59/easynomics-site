Privacy Policy

Last updated: 8 April 2026

Easynomics ("the app") is a free educational app that helps users explore financial concepts through interactive charts. Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what data the app collects and how it is used.

1. Data we collect

Almost none. The app does not require an account, does not use analytics, and does not include any third-party tracking or advertising SDKs.

2. Data stored on your device

The app stores a small amount of preference data locally on your device:

  • Selected theme and currency
  • Whether you have seen the onboarding screen
  • Whether you have accepted the disclaimer

This data never leaves your device. It is stored using your device's local storage and is not transmitted to any server.

3. Feedback form

The app includes an optional feedback form. If you choose to use it, the form opens your device's email client with a pre-filled message addressed to easynomics@proton.me. You may optionally include your email address for follow-up.

We only receive feedback you explicitly choose to send. We use it solely to improve the app and will not share it with third parties.

4. Data shared with third parties

We do not sell, share, or transmit any personal data to third parties. The app does not contain advertising.

5. European users (GDPR)

The app does not collect, process, or store personal data as defined by the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). No data is transmitted to servers, so no data processing agreements, data protection officers, or cross-border data transfers apply. If you have questions about your data rights, contact us at easynomics@proton.me.

6. California users (CCPA)

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), California residents have the right to know what personal information is collected and to request its deletion. The app does not collect, sell, or share personal information as defined by the CCPA. There is no personal information to disclose, delete, or opt out of.

7. Data retention

All locally stored preferences (theme, currency, onboarding status) remain on your device and are never transmitted. You can clear this data at any time by uninstalling the app.

8. Children's privacy

The app does not knowingly collect personal data from children. Since no personal data is collected or transmitted during normal use, the app is safe for users of all ages.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy if the app's functionality changes. The "last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at easynomics@proton.me.
